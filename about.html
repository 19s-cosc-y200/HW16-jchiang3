About Us

Pleased to meet you dear visitor!
Second Wind manifested from a desire to provide a place for individuals to gather and play games.
All the while we provide unique/foreign food and drinks for those to hang out to the fullest in this establishment.

Some Goals

  • To provide a location where people may congregate for recreational purposes
  • To provide unique experiences through food and community
  • To bring individuals joy and something to look forward to
  • Ultimately, to give people the needed Second Wind in life!
